Steps to make put in matches and make a ranking of a football league

  • The start is to create clubnames first. You need tot do that at the Home area, by clicking "Add Clubs"
  • The second step is to create a League, where you can put matches in later, at the League Area. Of coarse it's possible to create multiple leagues.
  • Then you can fill a league with matches in the Matches Area. Here you must first decide which League you choose if you made multiple leagues. A date has to be choosen, the system remembers the last date of input to prevent an input of the same date over and over again manually.There you add a club with an opponent. These are the clubs you added at the start. Then the amount of goals has to put in to make a result.
  • When matches are added in a League, it's possible to get a ranking of that league in the Ranking Area. After the League is choosen in this area it shows up.
  • It's possible to change club names, league names and match names by clicking on "Edit" in there area.
  • It's also possible to delete club names, league names and match names in there area. But that can effect a whole league and there ranking. When a club or a league is tried to delete, an overview of alle games that also must be deleted automatically is shown tot warn the user for the consequences.
